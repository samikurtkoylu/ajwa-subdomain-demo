AJWA Homes Istanbul

Craft and art come together with AJWA Homes. Fine craftmanship, aesthetic touches and a unique experience… AJWA Homes is a collection of several meticulously renovated classical Turkish houses that are converted into premium residences. The two luxurious units currently in operation feature four and three bedrooms, respectively, and generous living quarters. As fully serviced extensions of the nearby five-star AJWA Sultanahmet, they are available to hire for short and long term stays for guests who wish to explore the historical peninsula of Istanbul in the comfort and style such unique settings provide.

White Mansion

The first in a series of classic traditional houses in the Old City of Istanbul.

Literally meaning “white mansion”, this three storey, four bedroom classical Turkish house with generous living quarters has been refurbished to perfection, presenting a magnificent alternative for short or long stays in the heart of Istanbul’s historical peninsula.

The Clock House

Contemporary Luxury in the Old City of Istanbul.
The Clock House, the second in the series of AJWA Homes, owes its name to the clock on top of a brick building with smooth, curving features tucked away in the meandering streets of Istanbul’s uniquely quaint Old City.
